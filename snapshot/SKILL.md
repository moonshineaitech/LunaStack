# /snapshot — Quick Checkpoint

Every 20-30 minutes during active work:
```
── SNAPSHOT [time] ────
Done: [what just completed]
Next: [next action]
State: [Green/Yellow/Red]
Note: [one discovery, if any]
───────────────────────
```
4 lines max. 30 seconds.
