# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Be respectful and constructive in discussions
- Give and accept feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Credit sources when contributing protocols derived from other work

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Submitting protocols designed to cause harm or bypass AI safety measures
- Spam or low-effort contributions

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code of conduct. Repeated violations may result in a ban from the project.

## Contact

Report issues to the project maintainers via [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/moonshineaitech/LunaStack/discussions) or by opening a private issue.
